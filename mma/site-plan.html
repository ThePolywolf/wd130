Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provided major statistics for the upcoming UFC season in 2024, including top fighters, fighting styles, and a few of the top fights to look forwards to.

Audience

My audience is for MMA and UFC enthusiasts who are interested in major stats comign into the 2024 season, and alos for individuals interested in getting into watching UFC so they can know major events and stats to look out for.

Typography

Heading Font: Comfortaa, sans-serif. Comfortaa is a very relaxed font that will stand out from the body font well.

Paragraph Font: Mukta, Helvetica, sans-serif. Mukta is another sans-serif font, but is a bit more formal and tight without being an equal-space font, so it's very easy to read larger bodies of text.
