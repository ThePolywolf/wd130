Upcoming Fights

Stay ahead of the game with our list of upcoming UFC fights. We've got you covered with all the details you need to know to be ready for there major title matchups. Check back frequently for updates and don't miss a single moment of the action!

Vettori VS Allen

April 6th, Italy vs. United States

This is a matchup of two highly skilled and equally paired fighters. You don't want to miss this match. While this isn't the most monumental of matchups, it will be among the most entertaining fights this season.

Perriera VS Hill

April 13th, Brazil (-155) vs. United States (+130)

Periera is defending his title in this fight, however the odds are not in his favor. This will be a very technical matchup for the true MMA enthusiasts that you simply can't miss. Hill is likely to claim this title, however it is far from an easy fight for him.

Weili vs. Xionan

April 13th, China vs. China

These two female fighters will be a brutal match for both of them. Weili is one of the top fighters this season, and is likely to successfully defend her title in this matchup. Her fights are a must watch, and this one is no exception.
